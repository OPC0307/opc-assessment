Frequently Asked Questions

Can foreigners register an OPC in China?

Yes, foreign nationals can establish OPCs in China with a valid residence permit. The process differs slightly from Chinese citizens but remains accessible.

How much does it cost to set up an OPC?

Official government fees are minimal. The main costs come from registered address (if needed), seals, and optional professional service fees. Many subsidies can offset these costs.

How long does registration take?

Typically 2-3 weeks from document preparation to receiving your business license, depending on the city and completeness of your application.
