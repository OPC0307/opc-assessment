Registration Process: 3 Steps to Your License

1
Prepare Documents

Gather required registration documents. Requirements vary by city and registration type — we help verify everything one by one.

  • ID card / Passport
  • Registered address proof (lease or virtual address)
  • Company name (prepare 3 alternatives)
  • Business scope description
  • Registered capital declaration
2
Submit for Review

Submit registration application to the local Market Supervision Administration. We handle the entire process on your behalf, including follow-ups and corrections.

  • Online name approval (1-3 working days)
  • Submit registration materials
  • Market supervision review (5-10 working days)
  • Receive business license
  • Company seal carving
3
Bank Account + Tax Registration

After receiving your license, open a corporate bank account and complete tax registration. This is where many entrepreneurs get stuck — we guide you through the entire process.

  • Open corporate bank account
  • Tax registration and invoice application
  • Apply for eligible startup subsidies
  • Ongoing bookkeeping and tax filing services
