Shenzhen Startup Subsidies

Available Subsidies

One-Time Startup Grant
10,000 RMB/person; partnership up to 100,000 RMB
Eligibility: Shenzhen hukou or graduated within 5 years; registered within 3 years
Social Insurance Subsidy
Minimum standard subsidy, up to 36 months
Eligibility: Active operations + social insurance contributions
Rental Subsidy
500-1,560 RMB/month, up to 36 months
Eligibility: Leased business premises
Job Creation Subsidy
Up to 30,000 RMB
Eligibility: Hiring employees with social insurance
Startup Guarantee Loan
Up to 600,000 RMB individual, 5M RMB enterprise
Eligibility: Shenzhen hukou or graduated within 5 years; government interest subsidy

Application Process (General)

  1. Register your business license at the local Market Supervision Bureau (free, usually same-day)
  2. Operate normally and contribute to your own social insurance (at least 3 months for most subsidies)
  3. Log in to the city's Human Resources & Social Security Bureau website or government service app
  4. Upload documents: ID + business license + social insurance records + bank account
  5. After approval, subsidy is deposited to your personal bank account

Data source: local Human Resources & Social Security Bureau
