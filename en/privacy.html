Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

1. What Information We Collect

When you use the OPC Incubator ("the System"), we may collect the following information:

  • Basic Profile Information: Age range, gender, occupation status, education level, referral source (submitted only on profile.html).
  • Assessment Responses: Your answers and scores for the 15 assessment questions.
  • Phone Number: Collected only when you actively choose "Send full report to my phone" on the report page.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Anonymous visit data via Baidu Analytics (page views, time spent, referral source, etc.), containing no personal identity information.
  • Cookies: Used for language preference storage, visit deduplication, and other basic functions.

2. How We Use Information

  • Generate your OPC fitness assessment report.
  • Send the full report via text message (only when explicitly requested).
  • Optimize the question matching algorithm to improve assessment accuracy.
  • Analyze website usage to improve user experience.

3. Data Storage and Protection

Your assessment data and profile information are stored in browser local storage (localStorage) and are only accessible on your current device. We do not upload assessment data to cloud servers. Phone numbers are stored locally and only used when operators export data; they are not automatically uploaded.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party, except:

  • When disclosure is required by laws and regulations.
  • When you explicitly authorize us to share (e.g., sharing necessary information with service agents for subsidy applications, requiring your separate confirmation).

5. Third-Party Services

The System uses the following third-party services, which may independently collect information:

  • Baidu Analytics (hm.baidu.com): Anonymous analysis of website traffic and user behavior.

These services' privacy policies are beyond our control. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy terms.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to clear your browser cache and localStorage data at any time to delete assessment records and preferences. To confirm whether a submitted phone number has been deleted, please contact us via the methods below.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with the revision date noted at the top.
